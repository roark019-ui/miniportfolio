Career Interests

From the business fair on campus that I went to, there wasn't a company looking for a designer. However, there were a couple that were open to an HR Minor student. The two companies that interested me the most were Target and Marices. Target is a corporation that has a lot of posiions open frequently and I could work my way up. Maurices's headquarters are in Duluth so I could potentially work there since I do enjoy Duluth. Maurices is a clothing compay tha had broad reach but the company culture was more of a small business which I liked. Both companies had good benefits and seemed to value their employees.

Target and Maurices' both are bigger companies that didn't really have contact information to give out. They both had websites where I could apply for jobs and internships online rather that contact information for a specific person.

Detailed explanation of why you like that job/internship opportunity. I think working for a bigger corporation could get my name out there in a way, and it would be interesting to see the differences between bigger corporations vs small businesses in the way they work and the environment in the workplace. I think Maurices would be more my speed.

As of currently, I am not qualified for the positions as I do not have my HR minor yet, but I think I could be a good asset to a team as I am friendly, I like to work with people, and I like to help others and guide otherd to success. For a design position, I have some experience with graphic design through my coursework, a previous internship, and personal projects but I still need to build up my portfolio a bit more to be competitive.

My skills and interest could align with these companies because I love working with people and I enjoy collaborating on projects. I like helping people succeed and find their stride. I also have some skills in graphic design that could be useful for marketing or other design related tasks. I will gain experience and knowledge of more HR related topics as I finish my minor that will help me be more qualified for these positions in the future.
